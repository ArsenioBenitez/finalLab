  This correlation matrix shows the correlation between the homework grades of students.
  It's not a good measure for academic dishonesty since we are missing information. If two students that do all of their  homework have high
  correlation in homework and tests, that could mean they are both doing well in their tests because they do their homework.
  However, students with high correlations in test scores and low correlation in homework could indicate cheating.
